# Security Policy for EtnaMonitor

We take security seriously and appreciate responsible disclosure of vulnerabilities.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability, please:
1. Email security@etnamonitor.it with details
2. Include steps to reproduce if possible
3. Allow us reasonable time to respond before public disclosure

## Scope

Security issues affecting:
- Authentication and authorization
- Data privacy and protection
- Infrastructure and deployment
- Dependencies with known CVEs

## Out of Scope

- Social engineering attacks
- Physical attacks
- Denial of service attacks

Thank you for helping keep EtnaMonitor secure!
